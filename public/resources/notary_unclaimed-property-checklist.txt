Unclaimed Property Claim Checklist (Texas)
==========================================
From ReFurrm Mobile Notary - https://refurrm.com

Required Documentation:
-----------------------
☐ Completed claim form including Claim ID number (from claimittexas.gov)
☐ Copy of claimant's government-issued photo ID (driver's license)
☐ Proof of Social Security number or Federal Tax ID number (e.g., SSN card, W-2)
☐ List of all addresses used that may be associated with the property

If You Are an Heir:
-------------------
☐ Certified copy of the deceased owner's death certificate
☐ Probated will OR court order/affidavit of heirship

If You Are a Trustee or Guardian:
---------------------------------
☐ Copy of trust agreement OR current guardianship documents

If You Are an Executor/Administrator:
-------------------------------------
☐ Death certificate
☐ Letters of Administration or Testamentary (dated within 90 days of filing)

If You Are a Parent of a Minor Owner:
-------------------------------------
☐ Copy of the minor's birth certificate
☐ Proof of Social Security number

Process Notes:
--------------
• Attach all documentation to your claim form
• Keep copies for your records
• Submit via the state's unclaimed property portal or as instructed
• Additional documents may be requested by the Comptroller depending on property type
