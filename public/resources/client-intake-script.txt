Notary Client Intake Guide
==========================
From ReFurrm Mobile Notary - https://refurrm.com

To schedule your notarization, please have the following information ready:

1. YOUR FULL LEGAL NAME
   (Exactly as it appears on your ID)

2. DOCUMENT TYPE
   Examples: Deed, Power of Attorney, Will, Affidavit, Contract, etc.

3. NUMBER OF SIGNATURES
   How many signatures need to be notarized?

4. PREFERRED APPOINTMENT
   - Date
   - Time
   - Location (full address)

5. CONTACT INFORMATION
   - Phone number
   - Email address

6. SPECIAL INSTRUCTIONS (if any)
   - Hospital visit
   - Jail visit
   - After-hours appointment
   - Mobility needs
   - Other accommodations

IMPORTANT REMINDERS:
--------------------
• Have a valid government-issued photo ID ready at the appointment
• All signers must be present and able to sign on their own
• Travel fees will be confirmed before your appointment
